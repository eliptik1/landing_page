Various kinds of sea creatures

Clownfish are social fish, communicating through popping and clicking noises.

Jellyfish have no brain, heart, bones or eyes. They are made up of a smooth, bag-like body and tentacles armed with tiny, stinging cells.

Turtles spend most of their lives in water. They are adapted for aquatic life, with webbed feet or flippers and a streamlined body.

Sharks are a large group of fish that have inhabited the oceans for over 400 million years, even before dinosaurs populated our planet.
